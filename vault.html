  <!-- ======================================================

       WHAT THE BRAND VAULT IS

       ====================================================== -->

  <section class="section">

    <h2 class="section-title accent-line fade-up">What the Brand Vault Is</h2>

    <p class="section-desc fade-up delay-1">

      The VISIVA® Brand Vault verifies authenticity and ownership of critical assets

      (logos, master templates, sonic marks, films, guidelines). Each master is

      fingerprinted, registered, and tracked with immutable records.

    </p>

    <div class="three-grid fade-up delay-2">

      <div class="cin-card"><h3>Authenticity</h3><p>Each master asset is hashed and matched to its source.</p></div>

      <div class="cin-card"><h3>Ownership</h3><p>Auditable record of IP rights and transfer history.</p></div>

      <div class="cin-card"><h3>Provenance</h3><p>Full lifecycle: creation, approvals, issuance.</p></div>

    </div>

  </section>

  <!-- ======================================================

       HOW IT WORKS

       ====================================================== -->

  <section class="section">

    <h2 class="section-title accent-line fade-up">How the Brand Vault Works</h2>

    <p class="section-desc fade-up delay-1">

      The Vault combines fingerprinting, immutable records, and governance metadata to authenticate assets and permitted usage.

    </p>

    <div class="three-grid fade-up delay-2">

      <div class="cin-card"><h3>Fingerprinting</h3><p>Any file change produces a different hash — edits can’t hide.</p></div>

      <div class="cin-card"><h3>Registration</h3><p>Hash, issuer, timestamp, version, licensing/region/expiry recorded and sealed.</p></div>

      <div class="cin-card"><h3>Verification</h3><p>Guardian AI checks hash and metadata to confirm authenticity and rights.</p></div>

    </div>

  </section>
