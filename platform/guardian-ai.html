  <!-- ======================================================

       HOW IT WORKS

       ====================================================== -->

  <section class="section">

    <h2 class="section-title accent-line fade-up">How Guardian AI Works</h2>

    <p class="section-desc fade-up delay-1">

      Guardian AI converts standards into executable rules. Detectors evaluate assets and layouts,

      compare them to rulepacks derived from Brand DNA and guidelines, then propose or apply fixes.

    </p>

    <div class="three-grid fade-up delay-2">

      <div class="cin-card"><h3>Inputs</h3><p>Logos, colours, type tokens, layout specs, copy, context, licensing metadata.</p></div>

      <div class="cin-card"><h3>Engines</h3><p>Logo, contrast (WCAG), type scale, grid/spacing, voice, provenance.</p></div>

      <div class="cin-card"><h3>Outputs</h3><p>Compliance score, pinpointed issues, one‑click fixes, rationale, and audit trail.</p></div>

    </div>

  </section>

      <div class="cin-card"><h3>Inform</h3><p>Non‑blocking guidance during creation.</p></div>

      <div class="cin-card"><h3>Block</h3><p>Prevent non‑compliant assets in critical contexts.</p></div>
