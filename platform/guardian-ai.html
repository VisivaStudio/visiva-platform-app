Brand Guardian AI

Real‑time brand governance. Guardian AI prevents inconsistency, enforces rules, and protects brand equity across every asset and touchpoint.

What Brand Guardian AI Is

Brand Guardian AI is VISIVA®’s enforcement layer — a rule‑driven, context‑aware system that checks logos, colour, typography, layout, and language against the brand’s DNA and guidelines. It turns brand standards into living safeguards.

Live Compliance

Validates assets and layouts at creation and upload, not after publication.

Context‑Aware

Understands channel, size, medium, and accessibility requirements.

Actionable

Suggests specific fixes — not just flags — and can auto‑correct where allowed.

Why Guardian AI Exists

Protect Brand Equity

Prevents dilution through off‑brand colours, logo misuse, and unapproved typography.

Reduce Risk

Ensures claims, legal marks, and licensing rules are enforced at source.

Scale Consistency

Keeps distributed teams aligned without adding approval bottlenecks.

How Guardian AI Works

Guardian AI converts brand standards into executable rules. It evaluates assets and layouts through detectors, compares them to rulepacks derived from Brand DNA and guidelines, then proposes or applies fixes.

Inputs

Logos (SVG/PNG), colours, typography tokens, layout specs, copy text, usage context (web, print, social, product), licensing metadata.

Engines

Detectors (logo placement/clear‑space), colour/contrast (WCAG), type scale & pairing, grid/spacing, voice & terminology, asset provenance.

Outputs

Compliance score, pinpointed issues, one‑click fixes, rationale, diffs, and audit trail — all feeding BIE, Health Check, and Timeline.

Enforcement Modes

Inform

Non‑blocking guidance, highlights, and suggestions during creation.

Block

Prevents use of non‑compliant assets in critical contexts (e.g., press kits).

Auto‑Fix

Applies safe corrections: contrast uplift, logo sizing, grid snap, TM/® marks.

What Guardian AI Checks

Visual System

Logo integrity and placement, min size, clear‑space, protected area, lockups, colour space, and rasterization warnings.

Colour & Accessibility

Palette conformance, tint/gradient rules, and WCAG contrast for UI and type.

Typography & Layout

Approved families, weights, scales, leading, pairings, grid adherence, spacing rhythm, and component tokens.

Voice & Copy

Terminology lists, tone bands, ban‑lists, reading level, and legal lines — aligned with the Brand DNA & Copy Tone Assistant.

Licensing & Rights

Trademark marks, licensing scope, expiry checks, and usage conditions.

Provenance

Asset origin and authenticity with pointers to the Brand Vault (Blockchain).

Status & Roadmap

Live

Logo rules, palette conformance, WCAG contrast checks, typography tokens, basic voice checks, compliance scoring, and suggestions.

Beta

Auto‑fix for contrast, logo sizing, grid snaps, and legal line insertion. Governance reports pushed into Health Check & Timeline.

Roadmap

Provenance binding with Brand Vault, advanced copy compliance, channel‑specific rulepacks, and enterprise API integration.
