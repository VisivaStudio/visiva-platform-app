Brand Guardian AI

Real‑time brand governance. Guardian AI prevents inconsistency, enforces rules, and protects brand equity across every asset and touchpoint.

What Brand Guardian AI Is

Brand Guardian AI is VISIVA®’s enforcement layer — a rule‑driven, context‑aware system that checks logos, colour, typography, layout, and language against the brand’s DNA and guidelines.

Live Compliance

Validate assets/layouts at creation and upload.

Context‑Aware

Understands channel, format, and accessibility requirements.

Actionable

Flags issues, suggests fixes, and can auto‑correct where allowed.

Why Guardian AI Exists

Protect Brand Equity

Prevent off‑brand colour, logo misuse, and unapproved type.

Reduce Risk

Enforce legal marks and licensing rules at the source.

Scale Consistency

Keep distributed teams aligned without bottlenecks.

How Guardian AI Works

Guardian AI converts standards into executable rules. Detectors evaluate assets and layouts, compare them to rulepacks derived from Brand DNA and guidelines, then propose or apply fixes.

Inputs

Logos, colours, type tokens, layout specs, copy, context, licensing metadata.

Engines

Logo, contrast (WCAG), type scale, grid/spacing, voice, provenance.

Outputs

Compliance score, pinpointed issues, one‑click fixes, rationale, and audit trail.

Enforcement Modes

Inform

Non‑blocking guidance during creation.

Block

Prevent non‑compliant assets in critical contexts.

Auto‑Fix

Apply safe corrections: contrast uplift, logo sizing, grid snap, legal marks.

What Guardian AI Checks

Visual System

Logo integrity, size, clear‑space, lockups, colour space.

Colour & Accessibility

Palette rules and WCAG contrast for UI/type.

Typography & Layout

Approved families, scales, pairings, grid/spacing rhythm.

Voice & Copy

Terminology, tone bands, ban‑lists, legal lines.

Licensing & Rights

Marks, licensing scope/expiry, usage conditions.

Provenance

Asset authenticity with pointers to the Brand Vault.

Status & Roadmap

Live

Logo rules, palette conformance, WCAG, typography tokens, scoring.

Beta

Auto‑fixes (contrast, sizing, grid, legal lines); governance reports.

Roadmap

Provenance binding, advanced copy, channel‑specific rulepacks, API.
