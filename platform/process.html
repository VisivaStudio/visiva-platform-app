The VISIVA® Design Process

A structured, strategic, and cinematic approach to brand creation — combining discovery, intelligence, design systems, and immersive storytelling.

Process Snapshot — February 2026

Current State

The delivery model now has clear stages from strategy through governance and iteration.

Primary Focus

Tighten review gates and measurable acceptance criteria at each stage.

Cadence

Reviewed monthly with release checkpoints and governance sign-off.

1. Discovery & Alignment

Every brand journey begins with clarity. We define vision, audience, tone, and desired emotional impact.

Vision Mapping

Purpose, mission, long‑term goals.

Audience Insights

Who the brand speaks to — and why it matters.

Competitive Landscape

Opportunities for differentiation and leadership.

2. Proposal & Onboarding

A structured proposal detailing deliverables, timelines, pathways, and system requirements.

Scope Definition

Crystal‑clear project phases and outcomes.

System Architecture

Roadmap for identity systems, tools, templates, modules.

Onboarding

Access to VISIVA® Experience App and collaboration tools.

3. Concept Development

Multiple directions rooted in cinematic identity, strategic clarity, and emotional resonance.

Cinematic Identity Exploration

Light, contrast, depth, spatial rhythm.

Design System Foundations

Grids, typographic scales, spacing, components.

Logo & Symbol Development

Shapes grounded in philosophy and precision.

4. Feedback & Iteration

Structured review cycles refine concepts into powerful expressions.

Review Workshops

Sessions focused on clarity and cohesion.

Iterative Refinements

Enhancements to motion, composition, colour, and tone.

Stakeholder Alignment

Ensuring every voice contributes to the creative strategy.

5. Final Delivery

Production‑ready assets, templates, documentation, and components — delivered with precision.

Brand System Package

Logos, typography, colours, templates, layouts, docs.

Guidelines

Interactive manuals via PDF, Notion, or web.

Asset Library

Organised, downloadable, and future‑ready assets.

6. Support & Aftercare

Ongoing support, creative evolution, and strategic assistance.

Monthly Retainers

Design, strategy, compliance, and expansion.

Quarterly Reviews

Brand Health Checks & BIE evaluation.

Evolution Roadmap

Planning for markets, campaigns, or platforms.
