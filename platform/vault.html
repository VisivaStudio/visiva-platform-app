What the Brand Vault Is

The VISIVA® Brand Vault verifies authenticity and ownership of critical assets (logos, master templates, sonic marks, films, guidelines). Each master is fingerprinted, registered, and tracked with immutable records.

Authenticity

Each master asset is hashed and matched to its source.

Ownership

Auditable record of IP rights and transfer history.

Provenance

Full lifecycle: creation, approvals, issuance.

Why the Brand Vault Matters

Counterfeit Prevention

Blocks unauthorised “look‑alike” assets.

Legal Confidence

Bind assets to trademarks, licenses, and contracts.

Enterprise Control

Know the current version and who may use it.

How the Brand Vault Works

The Vault combines fingerprinting, immutable records, and governance metadata to authenticate assets and permitted usage.

Fingerprinting

Any file change produces a different hash — edits can’t hide.

Registration

Hash, issuer, timestamp, version, licensing/region/expiry recorded and sealed.

Verification

Guardian AI checks hash and metadata to confirm authenticity and rights.

What the Vault Stores

Master Assets

Primary logos, grid sets, palettes, type tokens, sonic marks.

System Files

Guidelines, component libraries, motion rules, 3D/AR assets.

Certificates & Records

Authenticity certificates, approvals, transfers, revocations.
